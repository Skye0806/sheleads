Who we are






We are a group of previous program managers and facilitators. After experiencing the difficulty of assessing program impact, we developed SheLeads.

We provide particpants a safe and engaging space to share experiences. We provide organizations program impact metrics to maximize participant development and reach funding goals.




Program evaluation is critical to:




Share stories of women and girls



Improve initiatives



Fund gender equality efforts
